Meet Gaby!

Gaby Wormann was born in Germany in 1966. She frequently visits Switzerland, Germany and Portugal. Her main inspirations are writers Franz Kafka, HR Ginger and Pierre Matter.

In her artwork, she focuses on ideas reflecting humans interfering and modifying the natural world, which is reflected by her merging organisms with mechanical items and creating new hybrid life forms.
